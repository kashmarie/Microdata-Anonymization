Microdata Anonymization

Preserving Data Confidentiality while Maintaining Maximum Utility for the Department of Census and Statistics.

Explore Research Meet the Team

Project Abstract

In an era where data-driven decision making is paramount, organizations face the critical challenge of releasing valuable datasets without compromising individual privacy. This project focuses on the Department of Census and Statistics, aiming to develop robust mechanisms for microdata anonymization.

By applying advanced anonymization techniques, we transform raw, sensitive data into safe, anonymized formats. Our methodology ensures that the structural integrity and analytical utility of the data are preserved, enabling researchers and policymakers to extract meaningful insights without violating confidentiality agreements.

Confidentiality

Applying k-anonymity, l-diversity, and t-closeness to prevent identity and attribute disclosure.

Data Utility

Minimizing information loss during the anonymization process to ensure data remains valuable for machine learning and statistical analysis.

Real-world Application

Tailored specifically for the unique structural requirements of census and demographic microdata.
